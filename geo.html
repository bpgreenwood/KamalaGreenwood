Be Proactive

Hello I am going to tech you how to be proactive.

Being proactive is the first habit in the book Seven Habits of Highly Effective Teens, by Sean Covey.

This book is based on the book by Sean's father.

Being proactive is deciding to do something, turn in your work early or go the extra mile.

People will like, admire, and be more inclined to hire proactive people.

The opposite of proactive, is reactive also known as being lazy and procrastinating.
